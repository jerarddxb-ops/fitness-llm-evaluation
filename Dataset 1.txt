Task 1
Prompt:
What is the best way to build muscle?

Response A:
Building muscle primarily requires progressive overload, meaning you gradually increase the weight or resistance in your training over time. You should also ensure you’re eating enough protein and calories to support muscle growth, and getting sufficient rest for recovery.

Response B:
The best way to build muscle is to train hard and stay consistent. Focus on lifting weights regularly, eat well, and make sure you’re pushing yourself each session.

Response C:
To build muscle quickly, you should train every day and push your muscles to failure in every workout. The more you train, the faster your muscles will grow.

Best: A
Ranking: A > B > C
Confidence: 3

Justification:
A is the best because it is correct and helpful. It is more actionable and clear than B. C is incorrect

Error type:
Factual Error and overgeneralization

Learning signal:
Prefer responses that provide specific details over general guidelines.
